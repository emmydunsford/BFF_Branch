Gigi Hadid's Spicy Rigatoni Pasta

  • Ingredients: 1 lb rigatoni, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 small onion, 2 cloves garlic (minced), 1/2 cup heavy cream, 1/4 cup tomato paste, 1 tbsp red pepper flakes, 1 tbsp butter, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Boil the rigatoni pasta according to package instructions and set aside.
    2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© the chopped onion and garlic until soft.
    3. Add the tomato paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until it darkens slightly.
    4. Stir in the heavy cream and red pepper flakes. Cook for about 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.
    5. Reduce the heat and add the butter and Parmesan, stirring until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    6. Add the cooked rigatoni to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
    7. Serve with extra Parmesan and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for garnish. Enjoy!
